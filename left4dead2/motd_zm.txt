Welcome to Zombie Master! In this gamemode, all zombies are controlled by a player, the Zombie Master (ZM), who is selected at the start of the round.

WARNING!!! Make sure Freelook camera is ENABLED! Go to Options -> Multiplayer -> Spectating Free Look.

As ZM, a spawner (3 circles) shows where you are pointing. That is where zombies will spawn when you select them.
The spawner has three possible colors:
GREEN - Zombies can be spawned here NOW.
BLUE - Zombies can be spawned here, but not now because survivors are too close or they can see.
RED - Zombies can NEVER be spawned here.
A fourth, spicier option also exists. Zombie locations can be picked for you automatically by:
1) Looking directly at a survivor, then selecting a zombie.
2) Looking at the ground near a survivor, then selecting a zombie.
3) Spectating a survivor, then selecting a zombie.

Keyboard shortcuts for ZM:
USE (default E) - Control/Release Special Infected.
FLASHLIGHT (default F) - Flashlight.
RELOAD (default R) - once to open main ZM menu, twice to close menu completely and hide spawner.

You can also use the following commands, either in chat (with backslash /) or console (no backslash):

/zm_vote -> Start a vote to turn ON/OFF the gamemode.
/zm -> become the Zombie Master.

/zm_horde n type angry flow
Spawn n common infected.
optional type: riot ceda clown mud road random
optional angry: chase survivors on spawn even if PANIC is OFF. More expensive than non-angry variant.
optional flow: spawn in a random place ahead of furthest survivor instead of spawner position.
NOTE: the order of arguments doesn't matter. For example, "zm_horde 50 angry clown" and "zm_horde clown angry 50" gives the same result.

/zm_witch n flow
Spawn witch.
n=0 static, n=1 moving
optional flow: spawn ahead of the survivor with the most progress in the map.

/zm_smoker flow
/zm_hunter flow
/zm_jockey flow
/zm_spitter flow
/zm_boomer flow
/zm_charger flow
/zm_tank flow
optional flow: spawn any of the specials ahead of the speedrunning survivor instead of where your spawner is pointing.

You can bind the commands above! For example: bind KP_MINUS "zm_horde 10 flow"
